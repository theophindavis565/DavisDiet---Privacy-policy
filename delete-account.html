Delete Your DavisDiet Account

If you would like to delete your DavisDiet account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at customercare@davisdiet.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the phone number or email associated with your DavisDiet account

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your personal profile (name, phone, address)
  • Your plan orders and meal history
  • All data stored in our Firestore database associated with your account

What Is Retained

We may retain order invoices for tax and legal compliance up to 90 days after deletion.
