Privacy Policy – DavisDiet

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

DavisDiet respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, address, and gender
  • Location Data: To identify delivery location via map or GPS
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version (automatically via Firebase)
  • Order and Usage Data: Meals ordered, preferences, and delivery history

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver meal plans
  • To process and track orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To analyze and improve app features
  • To send important notifications (no promotional ads)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers or marketers.

We may share limited data with:

  • Google Maps (for location functionality)
  • Firebase (for secure authentication and storage)

4. Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Firebase services. Only authorized personnel can access user records.

5. User Control

You may request deletion or access to your data by contacting us at:

Email: support@davisdiet.com

6. Children’s Privacy

DavisDiet is not intended for users under the age of 13.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.

Contact Us: customercare@davisdiet.com
